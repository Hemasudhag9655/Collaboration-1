HARRY POTTER

The Harry Potter series, a globally beloved fantasy saga by J.K. Rowling, follows young Harry Potter, an orphan who discovers he is a wizard on his eleventh birthday and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Alongside his loyal friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, Harry faces the dark wizard Voldemort and his followers, navigating the challenges of his destiny and the magical world while embracing themes of friendship, love, courage, and good versus evil. The series, which includes seven novels, has sold over 500 million copies worldwide and has been adapted into a blockbuster film franchise.
